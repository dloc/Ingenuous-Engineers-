Vendor Services for Jonathan Dickinson State Park


River Store

Boat Tours, Food Service, Merchandise Sales, Recreational Equipment Rental

We offer canoes, kayaks (singles and tandem), stand up paddleboards, motor boats, and single speed cruiser bicycle rentals for your enjoyment inside the park. "Palmettos on the Loxahatchee" is our food and beverage garden located right next to the beautiful Loxahatchee River. Our food is made using local, sustainable or organic foods and we also offer beer and wine. For more information, please call 561-746-1466.
